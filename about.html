Hi! I am Som Roy

I am pursuing my masters in Interaction Design at National Institue of Design, Bangalore. I am spending my third semester as an exchange student in Utrecht, a beautiful Romanesque city in the Netherlands at Hogeschool voor de Kunsten Utrecht.

My motivation to join Interaction Design after graduating in architecture is the opportunity to explore fast-changing technologies and making them easy-to-do and simple-to-understand for us to benefit our life and society. My projects are my humble efforts to combine the physical world with the newly emerging digital world to create mesmerizing but meaningful experiences. My approach is research-driven primarily focussing on users needs, which helps me pursue an unbiased approach to the solution.

Besides IxD, I have a huge love for food which treats me to carry around a few extra pounds. It's also the reason why I hang out more in 'gullies, baazars' and 'chowks' because I will either be looking for some famous food joints or at an old mansion across the street. At work, I like taking ping-pong breaks and drink a lot of tea or 'chai'. I have been moving cities since I was a kid and now I stay in Bangalore, the city of lush-green gardens and IT tech-parks.
